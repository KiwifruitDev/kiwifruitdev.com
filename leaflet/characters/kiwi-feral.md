# Kiwi (Feral)

Kiwi is the main character of Leaflet, the player avatar, and the protagonist of the story.

This is the pre-Lightly version of Kiwi, where he was a feral (on all-fours) husky dog.

Denotation for post-Lightly Kiwi can be found as [Kiwi](/leaflet/characters/kiwi.md).

## Appearance

Kiwi has a dark brown coat with a white underbelly/chest that leads into his tail.

He wears a [nylon](/leaflet/worldbuilding/warnings.md) collar with a dark green metal tag that has his name on it.

## Lore

Kiwi was born on Earth and lived as a feral dog until he was brought to Lightly from a time travel incident.

He recalls details of his life on Earth, but he doesn't remember much about his owner.
