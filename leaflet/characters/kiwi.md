# Kiwi

Kiwi is the main character of Leaflet, the player avatar, and the protagonist of the story.

He is a husky dog who lives in the town of Nicea with his best friend Claude.

Denotation for pre-Lightly Kiwi can be found as [Kiwi (Feral)](/leaflet/characters/kiwi-feral.md).

## Appearance

Kiwi has a light green coat with a white underbelly/chest that leads into his tail and underarms.

He wears a [nylon](/leaflet/worldbuilding/warnings.md) collar with a dark green metal tag that has his name on it.

His light green fur is a side-effect of his magical abilities.

A natural fur color for Kiwi would be a dark brown, similar to his [feral counterpart](/leaflet/characters/kiwi-feral.md).

## Lore

As husky dogs don't normally exist in Lightly, Kiwi is a unique species.

Due to a time travel incident, Kiwi was brought from Earth to Lightly and was raised by Claude.

He was misinterpreted as a wolf, as Claude taught him that the word for wolf in the [native language](/leaflet/worldbuilding/language.md) is his species.

## Personality

Kiwi is a very friendly and outgoing dog who loves to make new friends.

He is a fast learner and is very curious about the world around him.

He is very loyal to his friends and will do anything to protect them.

## Relationships

Kiwi has a close relationship with his best friend Claude, who he met when he was a puppy.

He also has a close relationship with his [owner](/leaflet/characters/kiwis-owner.md), but he doesn't remember much about them.

## Abilities

Kiwi has the ability to manipulate time, which is how he was able to travel to Lightly in the first place.

The extent of this time travel ability is similar to the game [Life is Strange](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Life_Is_Strange), where he can rewind time to a previous point. He cannot travel to the future, only the past.

## Trivia

- Kiwi is named a kiwi fruit because both him and Claude's color schemes make up the colors of one.
- Kiwi is the only character in Lightly who possesses a magical ability.
