# Claude

Claude is the side-character in Leaflet, the best friend of Kiwi, and a major supporting character in the story.

He is a California sea lion who lives in the town of Nicea with his best friend Kiwi.

## Appearance

Claude has a light brown coat.

He wears a blue bandana around his neck with a yellow marking, resembling a [star](/leaflet/worldbuilding/warnings.md).

## Lore

Claude believes that he is the only sea lion left, as he has never met another one before.

He was raised by wolves at a young age, which is why he believes that Kiwi is a wolf.

## Personality

Claude isn't very outgoing, but he will do anything to protect his friends.

He is shy, anxious, and nervous around new people, which is why he doesn't have many friends.

## Relationships

Claude has a close relationship with his best friend Kiwi, who he had raised while they were both a young age.

## Abilities

Claude thinks he can determine fate, as he can see the future in his dreams.

He is aware that Kiwi can manipulate time, which is why he is so nervous about it.

## Trivia

- Claude is named after Claude Speed from the game [Grand Theft Auto III](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grand_Theft_Auto_III) and Clyde from [Discord](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discord_(software)).
- Claude does not know what a human is, as he has never seen one before. Therefore, he is very interested in hearing about them from Kiwi.
