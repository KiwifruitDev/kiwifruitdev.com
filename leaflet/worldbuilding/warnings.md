# Warnings

This is a list of warnings to be aware of when working with Leaflet materials.

## Lore-based

- Human-Animal Hybrids are not a thing, at all.
- There are no humans in Lightly.
- Animals are only on two legs in Lightly.
- Human-made effects on the world do not exist in Lightly.
    - This includes traits that are essential to the lore:
        - Nylon (Kiwi's collar, persists in Lightly)
        - Dogs (Kiwi, persists in Lightly)
        - Humans (pre-Lightly Kiwi's owner, does not persist in Lightly)
- Time travel follows the butterfly effect ruling.
- Chaos theory does not apply to time travel, it is linear.
- Time travel is the only magical ability in Lightly.
- Time travel is limited to the past, not the future.
- Time travel is limited to the same timeline, not alternate timelines.
- Lightly is not a different planet, it is a different timeline of Earth.
- Wildlife is only made up of birds and reptiles. Mammals must be domesticated and stand on two legs.
- Dolphins, whales, and most other aquatic mammals do not exist in Lightly. The only exceptions are sea lions and seals.
- All carnivore mammals agreed on a peace treaty to never eat other mammals.
    - This is the lore "catch-all" for why carnivore mammals exist in Lightly without eating each other.
    - They probably eat fish or bugs, maybe even plants. Not important though.
