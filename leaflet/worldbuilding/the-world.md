# The World

In Leaflet, the world is named Lightly and is represented by an alternative version of Earth.

## Geography

The world of Lightly is divided into three different landmasses:

- Starward (Northen Hemisphere, based on North America)
    - Notably cold, with a lot of snow and ice
    - Town of Nicea is located here, as well as the capital city of Grandia
    - Nicea is based on both Skagway, Alaska and Skjolden, Norway
    - Grandia is based on Anchorage, Alaska
    - The town of Nicea is located on the northern coast of the continent, while Grandia is located in the center
    - Main characters Kiwi & Claude live in Nicea
- Trailend (Equatorial, based on South America)
    - Known for trading outposts and the main source of food for the world
- Pyre (Southern Hemisphere, a combination of Africa and Australia)
    - An island continent, with a lot of volcanoes
    - Has a lot of desert and jungle
