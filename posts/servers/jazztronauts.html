Kiwi's Jazztronauts

Hi, welcome to my Jazztronauts server!

This is just a simple server that I made for anyone to explore this old gamemode.

There is no Discord, there is no forum, and there are no staff other than myself. I just host this server for fun.

Features

  • Jazztronauts gamemode
  • Custom chat HUD
  • Automatic bunny hopping (hold space to jump continuously)
  • Outfitter in C menu (enter Workshop URLs to change your playermodel)
  • Simple third person in C menu
  • Sit anywhere script (ALT + E)
  • InfMap (Infinite map base, in case you discover any maps that require it)

Schedule

In Pacific Daylight time (PDT), the server closes at 1 AM and re-opens at 8 AM.
