# Donations
Donations are used to fund the development of Wildfire branded Garry's Mod servers and to pay for hosting costs.

Donating $5 USD or more will earn you perks in-game as a token of appreciation.

Donating to Wildfire Servers can only be performed by contacting **Kiwifruit#2003** on the [Wildfire Servers Discord](https://discord.gg/5gX3rdymxx).

## Donation Perks
More perks may be added in the future as we continue to develop our servers.

- **PAC3**
    - You will earn access to PAC3 within Wildfire branded Garry's Mod servers.
    - By using PAC3, you agree to follow the [PAC3 rules](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1XTJpkBLdkm_Wc9AW5HEhnYB1W1go6C3imBiiA6fEiy8/edit?usp=sharing) or your access may be revoked.
    - PAC3 is a third-party library that is not affiliated with Wildfire Servers.
- **Builder Permissions**
    - You will be granted access to permissions equivalent to the *Builder Role* within Wildfire branded Garry's Mod servers.
    - These permissions grant you a higher prop limit and the ability to use advanced tools such as effects.
- **Commands**
    - You will get access to special commands exclusive to your role within Wildfire branded Garry's Mod servers.
    - These commands are not available to all players.
    - **bmrp_explode**
        - Explodes and flings your player in the direction you are looking.
    - **bmrp_smite**
        - Smites your player via lightning and flings them upwards.

Donation perks can be revoked and are subject to change.
