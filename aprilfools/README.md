# Wildfire Servers

This is the front for server rules and other information.

At *Wildfire Servers*, we host **Wildfire Black Mesa RP** and a [community Discord](https://discord.gg/5gX3rdymxx).

- **About Wildfire Black Mesa RP**
    - Black Mesa RP is a semi-serious RP server that is dedicated to fans of Half-Life. You can play as a surface citizen, a scientist, or perhaps as a member of the H.E.C.U. Taking place prior to the events of Half-Life, you can transport samples, teleport to xen, and sell crystals for profit. You might even encounter some random events along the way, such as a complete flooding or a total blackout. Have a very safe and productive day!
    - Screenshot by ClearlyNotAdam.
    - This server launched on Sunday, October 17th, 2021.
    - Join the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/5gX3rdymxx).

- **Contributing**
    - Discord members may receive a *Contributor* role assigned to them as a special thanks.
    - Check out our [GitHub](https://github.com/wildfireservers/).
